Disability awareness training

This is an all day training offer which is made up of three informative, interactive and insightful workshops.

Book some training

Participants will be able to experience trying some practical tasks as well as the opportunity to develop learning around different types of disability. With the opportunity to engage in a Q&A, participants will embark on a unique learning experience. Please see below for further details.

Session 1 - Disability and it's effects

  • General information, definitions, facts and figures
  • Medical and social models
  • Lived experience
  • Emotional impact of disability

Session 2 - Deep dive into different disabilities

Specific Information and Advice

  • Physical disability
  • Sensory disability
  • Neurodiversity
  • Learning disability/ difficulty

Session 3 - Disability in Practise

Practical tasks and interactive workshop

  • Sighted guiding and audio description
  • Hearing loss tasks
  • Physical Challenges tasks
