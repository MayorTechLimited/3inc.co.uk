Nathan and Phoebe passionately believe in raising awareness of and educating about, the emotional impact living with a disability can have. This includes acknowledging the challenges but also celebrating the many positives. In this honest and thought-provoking training, we share their own real, lived experiences about living with a disability and support the audience to start to think of actions they can take to start to change perspectives and if they are comfortable too, share their own experiences.
