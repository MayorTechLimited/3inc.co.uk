I offer 'Physical Disability Awareness training' through the lens of real, lived experience. This training has been created to support organisations and businesses on their journey to becoming truly inclusive of those who are physically disabled. It also creates the opportunity to start thinking more broadly about what being truly inclusive means and how this can support other types of disability as well as other protected characterises. Interactive and honest, this training can be tailored to meet the needs of your organisation and can be delivered on its own or a part of a wider training package. If you would like to find out more, please get in touch.

Disability Awareness Training

This is an all day training offer which is made up of three informative, interactive and insightful workshops where participants will be able to experience trying some practical tasks as well as the opportunity to develop learning around different types of disability. With the opportunity to engage in a Q&A, participants will embark on a unique learning experience. Please see below for further details.

Session 1 - Disability and it's effects

  • General information, definitions, facts and figures
  • Medical and social models
  • Lived experience
  • Emotional impact of disability

Session 2 - Deep dive into different disabilities

Specific Information and Advice

  • Physical disability
  • Sensory disability
  • Neurodiversity
  • Learning disability/ difficulty

Session 3 - Disability in Practise

Practical tasks and interactive workshop

  • Sighted guiding and audio description
  • Hearing loss tasks
  • Physical Challenges tasks
